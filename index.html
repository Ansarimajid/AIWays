The Field Explained

AI, ML, DL, GenAI — What's the Difference?

These terms get thrown around interchangeably, but they mean very different things. Here's the full picture.

Artificial Intelligence
The broadest umbrella — any technique that enables machines to mimic human intelligence, from rule-based systems to neural networks.
The Universe
Machine Learning
A subset of AI where systems learn patterns from data and improve over time without being explicitly programmed for every scenario.
Learning from Data
Deep Learning
A subset of ML using neural networks with many layers to model complex patterns — the engine behind image recognition and speech.
Neural Networks
Generative AI
The frontier — models that generate new content (text, images, code, audio). Built on Deep Learning. Think GPT-4, Stable Diffusion, Gemini.
Create New Content
